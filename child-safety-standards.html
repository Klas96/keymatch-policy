KeyMatch maintains a zero-tolerance policy for any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or grooming. We are committed to protecting minors and preventing harm to children through our platform.

1. Age Verification & Access Control

1.1 Minimum Age Requirement

  • Minimum Age: 18 years old (strictly enforced)
  • Age Verification: Multiple verification methods required
  • Documentation: Government-issued ID verification for suspicious accounts
  • Continuous Monitoring: Ongoing age verification throughout account lifecycle

1.2 Age Verification Methods

  • Self-reported age with verification prompts
  • Photo-based age estimation technology
  • Behavioral analysis for age consistency
  • Manual review for flagged accounts
  • Integration with third-party age verification services

2. Content Moderation & Safety

2.1 Prohibited Content

  • Any sexual content involving minors (under 18)
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming behavior or predatory language
  • Requests for explicit content from minors
  • Age-inappropriate conversations or requests
  • Attempts to circumvent age restrictions

2.2 Automated Detection Systems

  • AI-powered content analysis for inappropriate material
  • Behavioral pattern recognition for grooming detection
  • Image analysis for age verification and inappropriate content
  • Real-time message scanning for predatory language
  • Automated flagging of suspicious age-related conversations

2.3 Content Moderation

  • Automated content filtering and detection
  • Manual review of flagged content by the developer
  • Escalation protocols for potential criminal activity
  • Regular review of moderation effectiveness
  • Partnership with child safety organizations
