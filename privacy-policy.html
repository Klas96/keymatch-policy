Privacy Policy for KeyMatch

Last updated: June 28, 2025

Information We Collect

KeyMatch collects the following information to provide our dating service:

  • Photos: Images you upload to your profile
  • Location data: Your GPS location to find nearby matches
  • Profile information: Name, age, bio, preferences
  • Account information: Email address for account management

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Show your profile to potential matches
  • Find and suggest compatible users near you
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important app updates and notifications

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is only shared with:

  • Other users through your public profile
  • Service providers who help us operate the app
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of certain communications

Camera and Location Permissions

KeyMatch requests camera access to allow you to take and upload photos for your profile. Location access is used to find matches near you. You can disable these permissions in your device settings, but some app features may not work properly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
