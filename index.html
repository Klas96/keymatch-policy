About Key Match

Key Match is a unique connection app that matches people based on keywords. Share your interests, hobbies, goals, or any keywords that represent who you are, and discover others who share similar keywords.

Our keyword-based matching system helps you find connections through shared interests, experiences, or aspirations. Simply add keywords to your profile and let Key Match find people who share your keywords.

  • Keyword-based matching system
  • Share interests and hobbies as keywords
  • Discover people with similar keywords
  • Simple and intuitive keyword profiles
  • Connect through shared interests
